Asia Restaurant is an independently family owned and operated restaurant located in San Antonio,TX. Our goal is to provide our customers with good hospitality and authentic chinese cuisine in a family friendly environment. Asia Restaurant has been in business for over 15 years, opening in the early 1980's. We offer some of the best chinese food in town and our restaurant offers many different lunch specials daily. Lunch begins at 11 am and runs until 4 pm. Take a look at our menu or visit us for more details.
